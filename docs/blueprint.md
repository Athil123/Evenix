# **App Name**: Evenix Auto Solutions

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Display a hero section with a full-width background image of a car repair shop, company name, tagline, and a 'Book a Service' button.
- About Us Section: Showcase an 'About Us' section with a workshop image and a description of Evenix Automotive's expertise and commitment to quality.
- Our Services Section: Present an 'Our Services' section as a grid/card layout. List Automotive & Mechanical, Electrical, Air Conditioning, Welding & Fabrication, Computer Diagnostics, Radiator Services, Tire Services, and Oil Change.
- Contact Section: Contact section displaying the company address, phone number, and email address
- Footer: Implement a dark automotive-themed footer with links to main pages and social media.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#2E5266) to convey trust and professionalism, reflecting the reliability of automotive services.
- Background color: Dark Gray (#222222) for a modern, automotive-themed aesthetic.
- Accent color: Metallic Silver (#A8A8A8) used sparingly for highlights and CTAs, inspired by automotive metallic finishes.
- Font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif font with a precise, contemporary feel, suitable for both headings and body text.
- Automotive themed icons used throughout the application.
- Spacious layout with a professional, mobile-friendly design.
- Subtle scroll animations to enhance user experience.
